Goals

Goals are instances of the Goal class. Its constructor receives as a parameter the expression of the Goal.


        g = Goal("Var(O1,1,Int)")

When a goal is added, the BDIAgent will try to perform this goal by invoking services. Once the goal is completed, the goalCompleted callback is called to notify the agent. 

        $ def goalCompletedCB( goal ):
            print "Goal completed!"
            
        $ agent.saveFact("Value",0)

        $ agent.setGoalCompletedCB( goalCompletedCB )

        $ agent.addGoal( Goal("Var(Value,0,Int)") )
        > "Goal completed!"
